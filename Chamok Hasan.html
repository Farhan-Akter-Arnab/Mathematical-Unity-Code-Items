Chamok Hasan

Engineer, Educator, Musician, and Author

Chamok Hasan (born July 28, 1986, Kushtia, Bangladesh) is a multifaceted Bangladeshi engineer, educator, musician, and author. He is widely recognized for his innovative approach to teaching mathematics and science, blending education with entertainment to engage learners of all ages.

Early Life and Education

Chamok Hasan completed his B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). He then pursued a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of South Carolina, USA. Currently, he works as an R&D engineer at Boston Scientific Corporation, a leading medical device manufacturing company located in California. His research interests include MRI RF safety, electronically steerable antennas, and wideband metasurfaces.

Contributions to Education and Music

In addition to his engineering career, Chamok Hasan is a passionate educator and musician. He is known for his online educational content, where he simplifies complex mathematical and scientific concepts through engaging videos and songs. His YouTube channel features a variety of educational materials aimed at making learning enjoyable and accessible.

Publications

Chamok Hasan has authored several books in Bengali, focusing on mathematics and popular science. His works aim to demystify complex topics and inspire a love for learning among readers. Notable among his publications is "অঙ্ক ভাইয়া" ("Anko Vaiya"), a book that presents mathematical concepts in a fun and relatable manner, making them accessible to a broader audience.

Music Direction in Film

In 2022, Chamok Hasan expanded his creative horizons by debuting as a music director in the Indian Bengali-language drama film "Baba Baby O." His involvement in the film industry showcases his versatility and passion for the arts.

Online Presence

Chamok Hasan maintains an active online presence, where he shares educational content, musical compositions, and personal insights. His platforms serve as a resource for learners and enthusiasts interested in mathematics, science, and music.
