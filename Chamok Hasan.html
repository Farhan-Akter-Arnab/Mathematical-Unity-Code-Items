Chamok Hasan

Engineer, Educator, Musician, and Author

Chamok Hasan (born July 28, 1986, Kushtia, Bangladesh) is a multifaceted Bangladeshi engineer, educator, musician, and author. He is widely recognized for his innovative approach to teaching mathematics and science, blending education with entertainment to engage learners of all ages.

Early Life and Education

Chamok Hasan completed his undergraduate studies in Electrical Engineering at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), an institution widely regarded for excellence in science and engineering education. During his time at BUET, he developed a strong command over mathematics, electromagnetics, and analytical problem-solving, which later became central to both his professional and educational pursuits.

He went on to pursue a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of South Carolina, USA. His doctoral research focused on advanced topics in radio-frequency systems and electromagnetic theory, strengthening his expertise in areas that require deep mathematical modeling and precision. After completing his doctoral studies, he joined Boston Scientific Corporation as a Research and Development engineer, where he works on cutting-edge medical technologies. His research interests include MRI RF safety, electronically steerable antennas, and wideband metasurfaces—fields that combine applied mathematics, physics, and engineering design.

Contributions to Education and Music

Alongside his engineering career, Chamok Hasan is widely known for his contributions to mathematics and science education. He has gained significant recognition for presenting complex mathematical and scientific concepts in an engaging, accessible, and intuitive manner. His educational approach emphasizes conceptual clarity over rote memorization, helping learners build confidence and genuine understanding.

A distinctive feature of his educational work is the integration of music with learning. Through original songs and creative performances, he conveys mathematical ideas, scientific principles, and problem-solving strategies in a way that is both memorable and enjoyable. This interdisciplinary method has made his content especially popular among students, teachers, and self-learners.

Publications

Chamok Hasan has authored multiple books in Bengali that focus on mathematics, science, and logical thinking. His writings are aimed at a broad audience, ranging from school students to general readers with an interest in understanding scientific ideas more deeply. Rather than functioning as conventional textbooks, his books emphasize intuition, curiosity, and real-world connections.

One of his most well-known works is “অঙ্ক ভাইয়া” (“Anko Vaiya”), which presents mathematical concepts in a friendly and relatable style. In addition to this title, he has written several other books that explore mathematics and science from a popular-education perspective, contributing significantly to science communication in the Bengali language.

Music Direction in Film

In 2022, Chamok Hasan expanded his creative endeavors by making his debut as a music director for the Indian Bengali-language drama film “Baba Baby O”. This milestone highlighted his versatility as both a technical professional and an artist. His involvement in film music reflects the same creativity and thoughtful expression that characterize his educational and musical works.

Personal Life

Chamok Hasan is married to Feroja Binte Omar and is the father of two children. Alongside his professional, educational, and artistic activities, he maintains a balanced family life. His role as a parent further informs his commitment to education and effective communication, particularly in making learning accessible and engaging for younger generations.

Online Presence

Chamok Hasan maintains an active and influential online presence, where he regularly shares educational videos, musical content, and reflections on learning, creativity, and scientific thinking. His digital platforms function as open educational spaces, providing free access to high-quality learning resources.

Through sustained engagement and innovative presentation, he has built a wide audience interested in mathematics, science, engineering, and music. His work continues to inspire learners to approach complex subjects with curiosity, confidence, and enjoyment. One can even visit his YouTube channel: Chamok Hasan (Mathematical Channel)
