East Asian Mathematicians

East Asian mathematicians have made extraordinary contributions to number theory, geometry, algebra, and calculus. From ancient Chinese algorithms and calendar calculations to Japanese innovations in algebra and probability, their work has greatly influenced both regional and global mathematical development.
