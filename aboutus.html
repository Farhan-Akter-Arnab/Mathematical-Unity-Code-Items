This group is mainly originated in Bangladesh by some students who like Mathematics, Science and other things. We try to find out some interesting facts on Mathematics, Science and try to solve them. The topics discussed here range from Number Theory, Algebra, Geometry to Physics, Chemistry and other various things. Here we try to maintain that the topics do not become dull or uninteresting, but rather engaging to others. Horsey Math News are also discussed in our community. Those who are interested can join our Discord Server that one can find in the footer section.
